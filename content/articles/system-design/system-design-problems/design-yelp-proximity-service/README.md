# Draft: Design Yelp Proximity Service

Building a location-based business discovery platform.

## TLDR

- Geospatial indexing enables efficient nearby search
- Ranking combines distance with relevance
- Reviews require moderation and spam detection

## Outline

1. Geospatial indexing: geohash, quadtree, R-tree
2. Search: nearby search, bounding box queries
3. Ranking: distance, relevance, ratings, personalization
4. Business data: profiles, hours, attributes
5. Reviews: UGC moderation, spam detection
6. Photos: user uploads, business photos
7. Check-ins and activity
