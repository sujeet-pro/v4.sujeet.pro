# Draft: Technical Roadmapping

Building and maintaining effective technical roadmaps that align with business goals.


## Why Technical Roadmaps Matter

A good technical roadmap:

- Provides clarity on technical direction
- Aligns engineering efforts with business goals
- Helps prioritize technical debt vs features
- Enables better resource planning

## Creating a Technical Roadmap

### Step 1: Understand Business Goals

- Meet with stakeholders
- Identify key business metrics
- Understand growth plans

### Step 2: Assess Current State

- Technical debt inventory
- System capabilities and limitations
- Team skills and capacity

### Step 3: Define Technical Vision

- Target architecture
- Key technical initiatives
- Success metrics
