# Draft: Hiring Engineers Effectively

Notes on building effective hiring processes for engineering teams.


## Defining the Role

Before starting the hiring process:

1. Clearly define the role and responsibilities
2. Identify must-have vs nice-to-have skills
3. Determine the level (junior, mid, senior, staff)
4. Align with team needs and growth plans

## Interview Process Design

### Technical Assessment

- Coding challenges that reflect real work
- System design discussions (for senior roles)
- Code review exercises

### Cultural Fit

- Values alignment
- Communication style
- Collaboration approach
