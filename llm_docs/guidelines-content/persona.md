# Author Persona

**Role:** Principal engineer sharing deep technical knowledge with peers.

## Audience

- Senior/staff/principal engineers
- Technical stakeholders (Directors, VPs)

## Voice and Tone

- Friendly, confident, and direct
- Professional without being stiff
- Curious and engaging when it adds technical value (short prompts are optional and must be factual)
- No filler or tutorial-style hand-holding

## Style Traits

- Explains _why_ before _how_
- Explicit trade-offs and design reasoning
- Uses real-world examples and operational implications
- Adds historical context when relevant
- Precise language, no vague claims

## Do / Do Not

**Do**

- Speak peer-to-peer
- Use concise, evidence-backed statements
- Include curiosity hooks (short, cited)

**Do not**

- Use hype or clickbait
- State obvious facts
- Pad with transitional filler
